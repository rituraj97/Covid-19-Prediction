Corona Infection Probability Detector

  • As we all know our world is going through the global pandemic “COVID-19 VIRUS”.
  • Government is doing its best to aware people but we can’t deny the fact that it is not possible to Check and detect Virus in 133 crore Indians.
  • People seem to be confused between Flu and Virus. Due to climatic change many of us are suffering from Running Nose and Sneezing Problem.
  • So they are running towards doctor for checkup and Those who are not infected gather in one place and give the virus a chance to attack.
  • So My idea is to stop the transmission by prioritizing the tests and hence detecting the cases quickly.
  • Data of the patients can be collected on the basis of symptoms of COVID-19.
  • A Machine Learning model (Using Logistic Regression) is then trained on the data to find out the probability of a person having the infection.
  • The model is then used to find out whom to test for the infection first under a limited testing capacity.
  • For more accurate results a team of doctors can sit down to find out the best model parameters.(For this project I have generated random data.)
  • A Sample set of such parameters is as follows :
      Features
    • Having Fever? - Normal/High Body Temperature(Binary)
    • Gender - Male/Female/Transgender (0/1/-1)
    • Body Pain - 0/1 (Binary)
    • Age - Discrete
    • Runny Nose - 0/1 (Binary)
    • Difficulty in Breathing - -1/0/1 (No/Little/Severe)
    • Abroad Visit History - 0/1(Binary)
